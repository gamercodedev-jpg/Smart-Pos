The Three-Stage Cash WorkflowTo make your reports accurate, the system must track cash in three distinct states:Opening the Shift (The Float):When the cashier logs in, the first screen should be a "Start Shift" prompt. They must enter the amount of cash they found in the drawer.System Logic: Store this as opening_balance.Active Shift (Running Total):As sales happen, the system calculates the "Expected Cash." * Formula: $Expected = Opening Balance + Cash Sales - Cash Refunds$Closing the Shift (The Drop):The cashier counts all the cash in the drawer at the end of the shift and enters it.System Logic: Store this as closing_actual.
